The Bushwick Review is a literary and art magazine that weaves together the work of more than 20 contributors in each issue. Over the years, we’ve featured short stories, essays, poetry, photography, paintings, drawings, architecture, plays, comics (basically anything that can be printed on a black and white page). The current issue is always only available in print.

Our home base is Brooklyn, but our contributors live all over the world. We’ve been around for a few years and multiple issues, but we are interested in making art and writing that speaks to being alive in 2019.

The Bushwick Review is edited by Kristen Felicetti and designed by Tim Vienckowski.
